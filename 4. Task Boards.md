# Task Boards

Tasks are the central unit of the Mastertech workflow. Every TUR Sheet submission creates a Task. Tasks track the lifecycle of a customer interaction from the initial tech call through follow-up repairs, upgrades, and checkout.

> See [[3. TUR Sheet]] for how tasks are created, and [[0. Table of Contents]] for all documentation.

---

## The Three Task Views

There are three task board tabs, each showing a different slice of the task list:

| Tab | What it shows |
| --- | ------------- |
| [[My Tasks]] | Tasks assigned to *you* specifically |
| [[Store Tasks]] | All open tasks for your store |
| [[Completed Tasks]] | Tasks that have been marked complete |

All three share the same Kanban-style card layout — they just filter the data differently.

---

## Task Lifecycle

```
TUR Submitted  →  Task created (assigned to salesperson)
                       ↓
               Salesperson calls customer
                       ↓
           Sale / repair / no-sale decision
                       ↓
     If repair/sale: Task reassigned to technician
                       ↓
           Work completed → Task marked Complete
                       ↓
                  Moved to [[Completed Tasks]]
```

---

## Task Cards

Each task displays as a card with:
- **Customer name** and contact info
- **Salesperson / Technician** initials
- **Check-in notes** / recommendations summary
- **Hardware info** (if "Send System Info" was checked on the TUR)
- **Due date**
- **Task status** and any follow-up notes

Cards can be clicked to open the full task detail view where you can:
- Add notes
- Change assignments
- Update status
- View the full hardware payload

---

## [[My Tasks]]

Shows only tasks that are **assigned to your account**. This is your personal work queue — the things you need to act on today.

Use this tab to:
- See what follow-up repairs or upgrades are waiting for you
- Track the status of TUR tasks you submitted
- Monitor any store tasks you've personally picked up

---

## [[Store Tasks]]

Shows **all open tasks** for your store, regardless of who they're assigned to. Useful for:
- Store managers to see the full workload
- Picking up tasks from a coworker who's out
- Getting a bird's-eye view of what's in progress

A **store selector** in the context menu lets you switch between stores if you have access to multiple locations.

---

## [[Completed Tasks]]

Shows tasks that have been **marked as complete**. These are archived but still searchable, useful for:
- Looking up past customer history
- Reviewing what was done on a previous visit
- QC reference after a sale

Completed tasks are fetched from the database on demand — the list is not live-updated automatically.
