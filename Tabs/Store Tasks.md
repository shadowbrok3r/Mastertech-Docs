# Store Tasks

Store Tasks shows **all open tasks for your store** — every active TUR, repair, and follow-up regardless of who it's assigned to. This is the bird's-eye view for the whole store's workload.

> Shared tab — available in both `Mastertech.exe` and at [master-tech.app](https://master-tech.app). See [[4. Task Boards]] for the full task system overview.

---

## What It Shows

All tasks for your current store that are not yet completed. This includes:
- TUR tasks waiting for a salesperson call
- Repairs in progress
- Sales follow-ups
- Tasks assigned to any technician or salesperson in the store

---

## Store Selector

If you have access to multiple stores, a **store selector** is available in the tab context menu (right-click the tab header). This lets you view tasks for a specific location.

---

## Use Cases

- **Store managers** — Monitor the full team workload at a glance
- **Techs covering for a coworker** — Pick up tasks from someone who's out
- **Tracking progress** — See where everything is in the workflow without asking around
- **Identifying stuck tasks** — Spot tasks that haven't moved in a while

---

## Sorting and Filtering

Tasks can be sorted and filtered in Store Tasks to help find what you're looking for. Common filters:
- By salesperson (to see all of one rep's open tasks)
- By technician
- By due date
- By status
