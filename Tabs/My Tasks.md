# My Tasks

My Tasks is your **personal work queue** — it shows all tasks that are currently assigned to your account. This is where you'll find TUR follow-ups, repair assignments, and any store tasks that have been sent to you.

> Shared tab — available in both `Mastertech.exe` and at [master-tech.app](https://master-tech.app). See [[4. Task Boards]] for the full task system overview.

---

## What It Shows

My Tasks filters the task database to only show tasks where **you are the assigned technician or salesperson**. This includes:

- TUR sheets you submitted (where you're the technician)
- Tasks re-assigned back to you by a salesperson after a sale
- Any manually created tasks assigned to your initials
- Store tasks you've personally picked up

---

## Task Card Layout

Each task is displayed as a card with:
- **Customer name** and contact info
- **Salesperson / Technician** — who created it and who it's assigned to
- **Service notes / recommendations** summary
- **Hardware info** (if submitted with Send System Info)
- **Due date**
- **Status** and recent activity

Click any card to open the full task detail view to add notes, reassign, or update status.

---

## Marking Tasks Complete

When a task is done — the customer picked up their machine, a sale closed, or a service was completed — mark it complete from the task detail view. It will move to [[Completed Tasks]] and leave your queue.
