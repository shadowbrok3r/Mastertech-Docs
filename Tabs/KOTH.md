# KOTH (King of the Hill)

KOTH — King of the Hill — is a **sales leaderboard** that ranks employees by their order and payment totals. It's a competitive view of sales performance across the team.

> Shared tab — available in both `Mastertech.exe` and at [master-tech.app](https://master-tech.app). See [[0. Table of Contents]].

---

## Views

KOTH has two perspectives:

### Me
Shows your own stats compared to the current period's leaderboard — your rank, order count, and payment total relative to your peers.

### All Employees
Shows the **full leaderboard** — every employee ranked by their performance metric for the current period. This is the competitive view.

---

## What It Tracks

| Metric | Description |
| ------ | ----------- |
| **Orders** | Number of closed/completed orders in the period |
| **Payments** | Total dollar value of payments received |
| **Rank** | Position on the leaderboard |

---

## Use Cases

- **Daily motivation** — Check where you stand on the leaderboard
- **Team visibility** — Managers can see the whole team at a glance
- **Friendly competition** — Who's on top this pay period?
