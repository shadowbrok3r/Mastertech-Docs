# Inventory

The Inventory tab gives a view of stock across company and store levels. It shows what's in stock, what systems are in the store, cost breakdowns, and lets you track changes in inventory as items are sold or received.

> Shared tab — available in both `Mastertech.exe` and at [master-tech.app](https://master-tech.app). See [[0. Table of Contents]].

---

## Stock Views

The Inventory tab has multiple selectable views via the `StockSelection` filter:

| View | What it shows |
| ---- | ------------- |
| **Company Stock** | Overall company-level inventory |
| **Store Inventory** | Stock specific to your store location |
| **Cost Breakdown** | Per-item cost analysis |
| **Systems In-Store** | Computers and devices currently in the store |

---

## What Each Table Shows

### Store Inventory
- Item name / SKU
- Quantity on hand
- Location / shelf
- Last updated

### Systems In-Store
- Computer make / model
- Serial number
- Status (available, sold, repair, hold)
- Price

### Cost Breakdown
- Item cost vs. sell price
- Margin calculations

---

## Customer Change Modals

Certain inventory actions (like associating a system with a customer) open modal dialogs for additional details. These are context-sensitive based on the selected inventory item.

---

## Use Cases

- **Before ordering a part** — check [[Part Order]] vs. checking Inventory first to see if it's already in stock
- **Checking what systems are available** for a customer upgrade
- **Tracking stock discrepancies**
