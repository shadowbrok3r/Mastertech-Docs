# Completed Tasks

Completed Tasks is the **archive of closed tasks** for your store. Tasks move here after they've been marked complete and serve as a searchable history of past customer interactions, services, and sales.

> Shared tab — available in both `Mastertech.exe` and at [master-tech.app](https://master-tech.app). See [[4. Task Boards]] for the full task system overview.

---

## What It Shows

All tasks that have been **marked as complete** for your store. Completed tasks retain all their original data:
- Customer info
- Service notes and recommendations
- Hardware information
- Who the technician and salesperson were
- When it was created and completed

---

## Use Cases

- **Customer history** — Look up what was done for a customer on a previous visit
- **QC reference** — Review the notes from a recent service
- **Dispute resolution** — Check the original TUR details if there's a question about what was recommended
- **Training reference** — See examples of well-written TUR notes

---

## Loading Behavior

Completed tasks are **fetched from the database on demand** — the list does not live-update automatically. Use the refresh button to fetch the latest completed tasks.

> ⚠️ The completed tasks list can grow large over time. Use search/filter to find specific tasks rather than scrolling through the full list.
